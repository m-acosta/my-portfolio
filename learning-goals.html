🎓 The Learning Spot – Game Learning Goals

Designed for young learners. Built for engagement, understanding, and confidence.

Flash Cards →

What Kids Learn:

  • Letter recognition
  • Number identification
  • Sight word reading

Skills: Reading Phonics Visual Learning

Rules:

  • Work with a grown-up to flip through cards together.
  • Cards may include: 🔢 digits (0–9), 🔠 uppercase letters, 🔡 lowercase letters, and 📝 simple sight words.
  • If you correctly name the item on the card, click the ✅ to celebrate!
  • If you’re unsure, feel free to skip and try the next one — learning is a journey!

Bingo →

What Kids Learn:

  • Listening comprehension
  • Letter-word association
  • Focus and memory skills

Skills: Listening Letter Recognition Focus

Rules:

  • Match the letter or word on your board with the one that is called out. Get five in a row — across, down, or diagonally — to win!
  • Turn your volume up for the best experience. Listening is an important part of the game.
  • You can choose to play using: 🔡 Lowercase Letters, 🔠 Uppercase Letters, or 📝 Sight Words

Story Builder →

What Kids Learn:

  • Grammar and sentence structure
  • Creative thinking
  • Story sequencing

Skills: Writing Creativity Language Skills

Rules:

  • Tap the blanks in the sentence to spin for new words.
    You’ll pick a character, an action, and a place.
  • Watch the magic happen! Your story will build right in front of you.
  • When you’re ready, click 🔊 to hear your sentence read aloud.
  • Play again and again! Every spin makes a brand-new silly story.

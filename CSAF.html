Cyber Security Awareness Fair

Cal Poly Pomona - Fall 2022

Team Members: Kalin Zaluzec and Juan Ceja Vargas

Annual fair hosted by Cal Poly Pomona.

Our group created an educational poster about the growing threat of ransomware and potential solutions. Attacks like these have occurred for over 30 years, and continue to evolve and pose a threat to companies and individuals. We wanted to educate others on this risk, and show a potential solution we found in a paper by Zhen Li and Qi Liao (https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0167404822000438).
